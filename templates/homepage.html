Features

Features like statistics, top keywords, and summary length selection provide advanced capabilities for the main content and summary sections.

Statistics

In the "Statistics" section, users can access information such as word count, character count, number of paragraphs, and number of sentences to gain insights into their text content.

Top Keywords

The "Top Keywords" feature allows users to identify the most frequently used keywords within their text content. This helps users choose the right keywords for a more effective and accurate summary.

Summary Length Selection

The "Summary length selection" feature empowers users to tailor their summary length according to their needs. Users can choose from options like one-third summary, two-thirds summary, or full-length summary, ensuring optimal content extraction.
